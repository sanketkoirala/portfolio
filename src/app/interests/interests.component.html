Interest /Hobies

Soccer

Soccer has been an integral part of my life since I was a child, and my love for the beautiful game has only grown stronger with time. I've been playing the sport for as long as I can remember, and it's on the field where I truly come alive, feeling the adrenaline rush with every pass and goal. My admiration for the legendary Lionel Messi and my unwavering support for FC Barcelona stem from a deep-seated passion for the sport. The way Messi effortlessly glides past defenders and orchestrates the game with his genius is awe-inspiring, and the history and spirit of FC Barcelona resonate with me on a profound level. Witnessing Messi play in person remains one of the most unforgettable and cherished moments of my life. As a devoted soccer fan, watching a match, I find sheer joy, and a sense of belonging. Soccer is not just a game; it's a lifelong love affair that fills my heart with excitement, purpose, and boundless happiness.

Photography

I love photography because it enables me to capture and preserve fleeting moments in time, turning them into enduring works of art. With each click of the shutter, I have the power to freeze a unique perspective, an emotion, or a breathtaking scene, allowing me to convey stories and emotions through images. Photography grants me the privilege of seeing the world through a different lens, quite literally, and it challenges me to seek beauty in the ordinary. It's a medium that constantly encourages me to explore my creativity, experiment with light, composition, and perspective, and find unique angles to convey the essence of the subjects I'm photographing.

Embedded systems and IoT have always intrigued me. I've been eager to explore the fundamental components of computer systems and turn my dream into reality one day. In the meantime, I enjoy working on exciting projects with my Raspberry Pi, taking small steps towards my passion. Each project brings me closer to achieving my dream, and I'm excited to see where this journey leads. The thrill of creating innovative gadgets and the promise of a future filled with cutting-edge technology inspire me to keep learning and growing in this exciting field.

Travelling

I love traveling because it is a transformative journey of self-discovery and an exploration of the world's diverse wonders. It's a captivating blend of adventure, learning, and experiencing different cultures, foods, and landscapes. Travel opens my eyes to new perspectives, challenges my comfort zone, and broadens my horizons, fostering a deep appreciation for the beauty and complexity of our planet.
